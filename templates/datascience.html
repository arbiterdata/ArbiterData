Analytics

Businesses today can generate and store vast amounts of sales, marketing and other kinds of data.

The challenge for many businesses is converting this data into insight.

Arbiter Data can process and analyse vast amounts of data to provide insight to management teams. For example:

  • at what times are sales made by month, week, day, hour, minute?
  • which products are sold with each other?
  • when do return customers return?

And so on and so forth.

Statistics

Statistics such as averages are a core part of virtually all management reporting.

However few businesses utilise techniques such as:

  • regression analysis
  • hypothesis testing
  • variance analysis

At Arbiter Data we can run statistical analysis to help management teams apply scientific techniques to their data.

Financial Modelling

  • how fast does this need to happen?

Such questions are often associated with understanding and assessing business decisions.
